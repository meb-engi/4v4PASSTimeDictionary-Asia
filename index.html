Welcome to the ASIA + OCE 4v4 PASS Time Website!

This website and it's Youtube Channel were created to promote 4v4 PASS Time for both the Asian and Oceanian regions. Both regions are very close and players from either play on the other very often due to low ping differences. between regions.

What is 4v4 PASS Time?

4v4 PASS Time is a fast paced basketball-like gamemode where two teams of 4 fight for possession of the jack and use a combination of movement tech and teamwork to score goals.

Video from passtime.tf

It is very different from other TF2 competitive modes such as 6s and Highlander with its unique class and weapon restrictions, effectively being its own seperate thing. It's getting its own source mod, which you can learn more about at the PASS Fortress Website.
